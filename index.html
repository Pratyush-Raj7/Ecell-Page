About Us

Enigma E-Cell at NMIT is a student-driven initiative fostering innovation, entrepreneurship, and leadership. Through workshops, events, and mentorship, we equip students with skills and confidence to transform ideas into impactful ventures.

We promote curiosity, collaboration, and practical learning. From ideation sessions to bootcamps and networking with industry experts, Enigma provides a platform for students to experiment, learn, and grow as future leaders and innovators.
